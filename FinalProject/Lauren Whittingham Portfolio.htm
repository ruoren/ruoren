Workdom


Workdom is a consulting firm that helps businesses grow. They provide services such as marketing, website redesign, rebranding, and customer growth plans.


Products

There mobile and desktop site has two users:

  1. Clients who talk to their personal consultant through the interface.
  2. Consultants who need to have the funtionality to do things like create calendars, and set deadlines.

Customers

Some of the customers they are trying to target are:


  1. Salons
  2. Real Estate Agents
  3. Lawyers
  4. And other small businesses

Goals

The Goals of my employment here were to:

  1. Create better marketing materials.
  2. Create templates for companies to use for their new websites.
  3. Building out the mobile consultant facing aspect of their website.
