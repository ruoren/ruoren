ANATOMAGE


Anatomage


Anatomage is a medical and dental device/software company. The purpose of this conceptual project to redesign their homepage to better present their products.


Products

Anatomage has two main products that they must showcase on their homepage:

  1. They create medical tables, which are tools used by doctors and schools to view anatomy in an interactive way.
  2. They create software for dental procedures such as implant surgery, and implant guides for the procedure.

Customers

There are two main customers to consider when designing the homepage:


  1. Doctors and medical schools.
  2. Dentists.

Goals

The goal of this redesign is to:

  1. Better display the two different departments (Medical and Dental).
  2. Give more insight into what the products are for each department.
  3. Make the homepage more aesthetic and easier to navigate.

Overview

Role

  • Acted as sole visual designer.
  • Redesigned the homepage of previous employer.

Tools

  • Low Fidelity- Pen and Paper sketches
  • High Fidelity- Sketch

Methodologies

  • Peer Feedback
  • Heuristic Evaluation

This will be done with keeping in mind the company preferences, such as logo, color scheme, and imagery.
